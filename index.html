Corpus is a language documentation notebook for doing linguistic data analysis that is intended to be aproachable for a non-technical user.

Thanks for checking this project out. We want to build a platform agnostic and (one day) feature rich alternative to FLEx with Polymer and Electron. We're calling this tool Corpus most of the time, and Glossa sometimes too.

Currently, the standard software for lexicography is FLEx, and... well... nothing else. this is SIL's Application for Windows/.NET, and is clunky and old... but does work. The app is free, and open source!

The FLEx (FieldWorks) Application does everything we need it to do, but is dated, and in desperate need of an overhaul.

See our Proto.io Prototype for a really rough UX Mock-up of what we are imagining... a sleek, refined user experience across platforms, and even online. One codebase and as future-proof as possible.

Thanks again for your time. Check out our wiki for more information, screenshots, and resources.
